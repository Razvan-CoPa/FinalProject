Intensive Accounting Course

Get certified as an accountant with a practical, hands-on approach.

About the Course

This intensive accounting course includes three modules: Primary Accounting, Financial Accounting, and Computerized Accounting. It is designed to give you a deep understanding of accounting principles, from basic documentation to using specialized software like SAGA.

Course Modules

Primary Accounting

Learn to complete and verify accounting documents, understand the legal basis for processing, and master archiving requirements.

Financial Accounting

  • Analyze accounting documents
  • Prepare accounting records, trial balances, and financial statements
  • Submit financial declarations

Computerized Accounting

Gain practical experience using SAGA, a specialized accounting management software.
